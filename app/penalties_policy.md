# UMOJA FEMSPACE PENALTIES POLICY

## Purpose
This policy outlines penalties applicable to late loan repayments.

---

## SME Loans Below KES 5,000

### Repayment Method
- Daily installments

### Grace Period
- 7 Days

### Penalty
- KES 100 per missed installment after the 7-day grace period.

---

## SME Loans Above KES 5,000 Up To KES 100,000

### Repayment Method
- Weekly installments (KES 5,001 – KES 49,999)
- Monthly installments (KES 50,000 – KES 100,000)

### Penalty
- 2% of the outstanding balance after the loan has fully matured and remains unpaid.

---

## School Fees Loans

### Repayment Method
- Monthly installments

### Penalty
- 2% of the outstanding balance after maturity.

---

## Emergency Loans

### Repayment Method
- Monthly installments

### Penalty
- 2% of the outstanding balance after maturity.

---

## Loan Status Definitions

### Active
Loan is being repaid according to schedule.

### Overdue
A scheduled repayment has been missed.

### Matured
The loan has reached its final repayment date.

### Defaulted
The loan remains unpaid after maturity and recovery actions have commenced.
