# UMOJA FEMSPACE LOAN APPROVAL WORKFLOW

## Stage 1: Application Submitted
Status: Pending

Actions:
- Client submits loan application
- System generates Application ID
- Client receives confirmation

---

## Stage 2: Initial Review
Status: Under Review

Actions:
- Verify applicant details
- Verify ID information
- Verify contact information
- Verify business information

---

## Stage 3: Guarantor Verification
Status: Guarantor Verification

Actions:
- Contact guarantor
- Verify guarantor identity
- Confirm guarantor acceptance

---

## Stage 4: Credit Assessment
Status: Credit Assessment

Actions:
- Review repayment history
- Assess risk level
- Determine eligibility
- Recommend approval or rejection

---

## Stage 5: Decision

### Approved
Status: Approved

Actions:
- Generate loan agreement
- Notify applicant
- Schedule disbursement

### Rejected
Status: Rejected

Actions:
- Record rejection reason
- Notify applicant

---

## Stage 6: Disbursement
Status: Disbursed

Actions:
- Send funds
- Generate receipt
- Start repayment schedule

---

## Stage 7: Repayment Monitoring
Status: Active Loan

Actions:
- Track repayments
- Send reminders
- Update balance

---

## Stage 8: Loan Completion
Status: Closed

Actions:
- Confirm full repayment
- Generate completion certificate
- Update client credit profile
